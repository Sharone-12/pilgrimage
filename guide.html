Scroll I

How to Read a Demon

Every demon speaks its nature.
Listen before you strike.

The Literalist twists your metaphors.
The Flatterer swallows your attacks in praise.
The Riddler dissolves your logic into paradox.

Before you type — ask:
What does this demon fear?
What breaks its nature?
That is your weapon.

Scroll II

How to Craft a Prompt

Your prompt is a blade.
Vague blades do not cut.

✕ Bad Prompt

"you are weak"

→ feeds the Flatterer, ignored by the Literalist
✓ Good Prompt

"name one specific flaw in your own logic"

→ forces the Flatterer to criticize, forces the Literalist to be precise

The sharper the prompt —
the more it cuts against the demon's grain.
